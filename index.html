About

I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at the School of Informatics, Xiamen University (advisor: Prof. Jiwu Shu and Prof. Yiming Zhang).

My research interests include network and storage system, with current research focusing on the cloud storage and computing. I worked as a research intern at Alibaba Cloud Intelligence from 2023 to 2025, participating in several cloud storage-related projects.

I received my B.S. degree in Communication Engineering from Chongqing University of Technology in 2018, and M.S. in Computer Science and Technology from Xiamen University in 2021.
